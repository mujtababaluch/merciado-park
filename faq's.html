Know Before You Go

Find answers to some frequently asked questions that will help you make the most of your day.






What if it rains? ("Rain checks")

All Guests, Team Members and Management must pass through metal detectors at the entrance gate, and all bags, purses and coolers are subject to search.

What Can Be Brought Into Merciado?

We welcome you to bring your own strollers, wheelchairs and wagons into the park. Picnic baskets are welcome too; however, the meal must be only for your immediate family. Group picnics and catered events must be coordinated through our Group Sales Office.

Emergency Plan

Discuss ahead of time what to do if someone gets separated from your group. The Service Center, located near the Slush Factory at the entrance to Lost Merciado, or the entrance to Kiddieland are great meeting places. Take note as to what your children are wearing in case you need to describe them to a Merciado Public Safety Officer. Lost children should be told to ask for assistance from a Merciado Uniformed Employee. When our Team Members find a lost child, that child is taken to the Service Center.

Cell Phone Charging Station?

Located inside the Penny Arcade near the Jack Rabbit roller coaster.

How old must you be to work at Cliff’s Amusement Park?

To be employed at Cliff’s Amusement Park, you must be at least 15 years old.

Minors (anyone under the age of 18)

We strongly recommend that Minors (under 18) be accompanied by an adult. Park does not assume any responsibility or liability for unattended minors.


Do you sell gift cards/certificates?

No, but we do sell Cliff’s FUNcards! Purchase them at the administrative office, at any ticket window during park hours or by going to our online store.

Rent Wheelchairs, strollers and wagons?

At the main entrance, you can rent wheelchairs, electric scooters, strollers and wagons. A limited supply of these items are available for online advance rentals. Please note, advance rentals are nonrefundable.

What CANNOT Be Brought Into Merciado?

Alcoholic beverages, firearms, glass bottles and pets are not permitted on park grounds. Overtly offensive clothing such as, but not limited to, clothing with hateful messages, pornography, vulgarity, illegal acts, and /or divisive political messages. Also Note: Tobacco is not sold in the park.

Remembering Your Parking Spot!

Make everyone in your group aware of the location of your vehicle in our large parking lot. If you all discuss it, chances are one of you will remember.

Can I smoke at Merciado?

Inside the park, smoking is permitted in designated areas only. Once you’re here, consult the Park Guide for the various smoking areas in the park.

Can a different person use the second day of a weekend ticket than the person who used the first day?

No. Photos are associated with weekend tickets and must be used by the original ticket holder.
